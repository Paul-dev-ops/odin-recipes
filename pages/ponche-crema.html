Creamy Ponche Crema Recipe

December is here, and with the winter we also have this delicious potion of the gods


Ingredients

  • 1/2 litre of milk
  • 1/2 litre of heavy cream
  • 180 gr of brown sugar
  • 5 egg yolks
  • 1 tbsp of cornstarch
  • 1/2 tsp NutMeg
  • 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 180 ml Rum

1,2,3.....Alchemy!

  1. On a cooking pot, lets pour the milk following by the heavy cream in total, and 3/4 of the brown sugar... (we will use the rest later on)

  2. Once all previous items are mixed and added into the pot, lets cook it on medium heat until we reach 60c... be careful not to wait until the milk starts to boil.

  3. On a bowl lets have the egg yolks and rest of brown sugar mixed with a balloon whisk, Once the egg yolks start to look clarified and creamy at that point we're gonna need to pour the warmed milk mix little by little and keep stirring the egg yolks with the milk.

  4. then with everything mix at this point we're going to return to the stove and on lowest heat we will mix everything, without stopping for at least 20 straight minutes in order not to let the mix get lumpy.

  5. while we're slow heating the mix on the stove, we're gonna need to dissolve the cornstarch with 100 ml of waters and then add to the mix.

  6. Keep stirring!! we're almost done!

  7. this is the momment to add all the spices and keep stirring!

  8. once the mixed gets thickened, turn off the heat and put it on a ventilated space so it starts gets to room temperature.

  9. Once the mix is at room temperature, add the rum

  10. put all the ponche crema liquor on crystal bottles

  11. let the bottle of that delicious potion rest for at least 12 hours

  12. Served with some ice cubes, and enjoy!
